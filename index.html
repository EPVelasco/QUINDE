QUINDE

Quadcopter for Unsupervised INspections in Dynamic Environments. Based on the DJI Matrice 100, Quinde is equipped with a Jetson Orin-NX with 16GB of RAM, an Intel® RealSense™ D435i RGB-D camera, an OpenMV Cam H7 thermal camera, and a Livox Mid-40 3D LiDAR sensor. It has been tested in real-world scenarios, such as photovoltaic array inspections, demonstrating its versatility and effectiveness in autonomous operations.
